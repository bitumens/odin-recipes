Chicken Korma

Ingredients:

  • Onion and garlic
  • Olive oil, salt and pepper
  • Rice
  • Korma sauce
  • Chicken
  • Chopped peanuts
    •

    Method:

    1. Begin by starting the rice according to the instructions on the packet
    2. In a frying pan, heat some oil on a medium heat
    3. Once hot, add the onion and garlic (being careful not to burn the shit out of the garlic) and sprinkle on some salt and pepper
    4. After a couple of minutes, add the chicken and fry until golden
      • Important note: Raw chicken is dangerous. Make sure your chicken is cooked all the way through.
    5. Once the chicken has cooked, add the korma sauce to pan and simmer for 8-12 minutes on a lower heat
    6. Once both the korma sauce and the rice are done, combine in a bowl and sprinkle on some chopped peanuts
    7. VOILA! You are done!
