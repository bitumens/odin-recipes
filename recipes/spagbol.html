Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients:

  • Onion and garlic
  • Olive oil, salt and pepper
  • Spaghetti
  • Bolognese sauce
  • Mince (I reccomend beef, but pork works too)
  • Cheddar cheese
    •

    Method:

    1. Begin by bringing some water to the boil in a saucepan according to the instructions on the packet
    2. Once boiling, add a pinch of salt and the spaghetti
    3. In a frying pan, heat some oil on a medium heat
    4. Once hot, add the onion and garlic (being careful not to burn the shit out of the garlic) and sprinkle on some salt and pepper
    5. After a couple of minutes, add the mince and fry until browned
    6. Once the mince has browned, add the red sauce to pan and simmer for 5-10 minutes on a lower heat
    7. Once both the spaghetti and bolognese sauce are done, combine in a bowl and sprinkle on some grated cheese
    8. VOILA! You are done!
